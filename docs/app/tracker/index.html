Add BowlsTrack to your Home Screen

Get the full app experience — instant access from your home screen, just like a native app.

  1. Tap the Share button at the bottom of your browser ( □↑ )
  2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Tap "Add" in the top right corner

Make sure you're using Safari — this won't work in Chrome or Firefox on iPhone.

  1. Tap the three dots menu (⋮) in the top right of Chrome
  2. Tap "Add to Home screen"
  3. Tap "Add" to confirm

BowlsTrack works best on mobile. Open this page on your iPhone or Android device to install it.
